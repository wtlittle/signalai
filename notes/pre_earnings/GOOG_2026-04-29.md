- Search disruption vs search enhancement: Is Google's AI integration (Gemini, AI Overviews) defensive or offensive? Bears argue AI reduces click-through to ads; bulls argue it increases engagement and query volume.

- Capex intensity: Alphabet's AI infrastructure spending is second only to Amazon. The market wants to see capex translate into revenue growth, not just cost inflation.
- Regulatory overhang: DOJ antitrust trial outcome and potential remedies remain a multi-year overhang.
