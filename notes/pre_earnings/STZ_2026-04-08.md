## Key Debates and Variants

1. **Beer depletions and volume health**: Modelo Especial is the #1 beer by volume in the US. The central debate is whether depletion trends (which lag shipments) are tracking at 5-7% growth or decelerating to 3-4%. Beer net sales guided to decline 2-4% for FY2026 (negative mix shift as portfolio premiumizes). Volume versus pricing is the key decomposition -- pricing power holding but volume softness would signal broader consumer trade-down.

2. **Tariff exposure -- Mexico beer is the entire moat**: 100% of STZ's beer is brewed in Mexico. With US-Mexico trade tensions and tariff escalation risk, the market wants a clear articulation of hedging strategy, cost pass-through ability, and multi-year CapEx plans for potential US brewing capacity. Any commentary signaling tariff headwinds above $100M/year would be a major negative.

3. **CEO transition narrative -- Nicholas Fink's opening remarks**: Fink joins as CEO on April 13, just 5 days after the earnings report. His opening remarks on the April 9 call will be intensely parsed for strategic direction: Does he validate beer-only focus? Does he signal capital allocation shift (dividends, buybacks vs. debt reduction)? Does he provide a fresh framework for FY2027+ that the street can build models around?

4. **FY2027 guidance and capital allocation**: The first FY2027 guidance issued under Fink will be the most important datapoint. FY2027 consensus: EPS ~$14.95 (per MarketBeat), which implies a sharp recovery. Finance estimates show Q1 FY2027 consensus EPS of $3.93 vs. Q1 FY2026 actual of $3.74 -- implying +5.1% EPS growth resumption. Any guidance below $14.00 would disappoint; above $15.50 would be a positive catalyst.

5. **W&S wind-down -- write-down and structural drag removal**: The Wine & Spirits segment is expected to generate near-zero operating income this quarter (consensus: $2.4M). Once fully divested, this removes a persistent drag. Investors want a clear timetable and any update on residual asset sales or restructuring charges.
