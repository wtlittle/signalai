## Thesis Impact

**Bull case reinforced:**
- Revenue growth reacceleration continues (12% YoY vs 10-11% prior quarters)
- Non-GAAP EPS growth of 19% shows operating leverage
- Firefly/AI monetization is scaling -- ARR 3x YoY
- 850M MAU provides massive distribution for AI features
- MLB partnership and enterprise deals expanding addressable market

**Concerns:**
- Stock sold off ~9% despite beat -- market wants faster AI revenue ramp
- UBS and TD Cowan both cut PTs — AI disruption narrative gaining traction
- Wide analyst PT dispersion ($235-$570) reflects deep uncertainty
- Valuation re-rating requires sustained acceleration above 12%
- Competition from Canva, Figma (now public), and AI-native tools
- GAAP margins compressed by stock-based compensation
- Recurring revenue growth rate a key watch item

**Net assessment:** Solid quarter with a beat on both lines, but the post-earnings drift to $246 shows the market is not giving Adobe credit for AI progress. At ~$246, there is meaningful upside to the $301 consensus PT, but the wide range of analyst views reflects genuine uncertainty about whether Firefly and GenStudio can offset the competitive threat from AI-native creative tools. The next quarter's AI ARR trajectory and enterprise deal conversion will be pivotal.
