## The Story of the Quarter

This was a definitive answer to the "peak AI spend" bears. High-performance computing and AI-related demand now accounts for 61% of total revenue, up from ~40% two years ago. The AI infrastructure buildout is not slowing -- it is accelerating.

Advanced nodes (7nm and below) represent 74% of wafer revenue: 3nm at 25%, 5nm at 36%. The mix shift toward cutting-edge nodes drives both higher ASPs and better margins.

Revenue grew 8.4% sequentially and 35% YoY in USD terms to $35.9B, slightly ahead of guidance. Gross margin expanded nearly 4 points sequentially to 66.2% on cost improvements, high utilization, and favorable FX. Operating margin hit 58.1%, up 4.1pp QoQ.

The EPS of NT$22.08 and ROE underscored the cash generation machine that TSMC has become in the AI era.
