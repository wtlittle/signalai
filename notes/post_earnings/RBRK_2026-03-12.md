# Rubrik Inc. (RBRK) -- Q4 FY2026 Post-Earnings Note

| Metric | Actual | Estimate | Result |

**Stock Reaction:** Despite the massive Q4 beat, RBRK stock fell 6.08% on earnings day to close at $57.52. Continued selling has pushed shares to ~$54 by mid-March — a 48% decline from the 52-week high of $103 and -29% YTD. Slight aftermarket recovery to $58.23 was short-lived. Market concern centers on valuation compression and forward growth deceleration.

## Key Operating Metrics

- Q4 revenue grew 46.3% YoY to $377.7M
- Subscription ARR: $1.46B (+34% YoY)
- Record net new subscription ARR: $115M in Q4
- Subscription revenue: $365M (+50% YoY)
- Subscription net retention rate: >120%
- Customers >$100K subscription ARR: up 25% YoY
- Subscription ARR contribution margin: improved 950+ bps YoY
- Q4 free cash flow: $70M
- FY2026 free cash flow: $238M (10x+ vs prior year)
- Cash: ~$1.4B in cash, equivalents, and marketable securities
- Convertible debt: $1.1B

## Guidance and Tone

**Q1 FY2027 Guide:**
- Revenue: $365M-$367M (~31-32% YoY growth)
- Seasonal downturn from Q4 as expected

**Full-Year FY2027 Guide:**
- Subscription ARR: $1,829M-$1,839M (+25-26% YoY)
- Total revenue: $1,597M-$1,607M (+27-28% YoY)
- Free cash flow: $265M-$275M

**Tone:** Bullish on data security market expansion, particularly around AI governance and Rubrik Sovereign Cloud launch. Management noted increasing enterprise demand for ransomware recovery and data observability. Go-to-market leadership transition underway -- management reassured smooth handoff.

## Thesis Impact

**Bull case reinforced:**
- Revenue growth of 46% with a path to 27-28% in FY2027 -- still hyper-growth
- FCF inflection from negligible to $238M in one year is remarkable
- Net new ARR acceleration ($115M record) shows demand is strengthening
- Data security TAM expanding with AI governance, ransomware, and compliance drivers
- >120% NRR means existing customers are spending more
- Stock at ~$54 trades at a meaningful discount to consensus PT of $91 — risk/reward improving

**Concerns:**
- Stock has fallen 48% from 52-week high despite massive beat — severe multiple compression
- FY2027 guide implies ARR growth deceleration from 34% to 25-26%
- Revenue growth estimates for FY27/FY28 revised to 22%/21% — declining trajectory
- Q1 seasonal downturn in revenue (Q4 to Q1) may create near-term overhang
- Go-to-market leadership transition carries execution risk
- $1.1B convertible debt creates dilution risk
- Operating margin adjusted to -1.3% loss, down from previously expected gain

**Net assessment:** Rubrik delivered an outstanding Q4 on every metric. However, the severe post-earnings selloff to $54 reflects a market that is pricing in meaningful deceleration and demanding margin proof. The wide gap between the stock price ($54) and consensus PT ($91) suggests either a significant re-rating opportunity or that street estimates need to come down further. At 9x forward P/S, the risk/reward is compelling for investors with a 12-18 month horizon who believe the 25%+ ARR growth guide is conservative.
